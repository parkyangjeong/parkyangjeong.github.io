Join Dr. Yang Jeong Park's Research Group

I am seeking highly motivated and talented individuals to join us as PhD students and Postdoctoral Fellows. We are passionate about pioneering next-generation materials and leveraging advanced computational tools to address global challenges in energy, environment, and beyond.

For Postdoctoral Fellows (Key Qualifications)

  • A proven track record of high-quality research, demonstrated by publications in reputable journals.
  • Expertise in experimental materials synthesis/characterization OR computational materials science/AI/ML. Candidates with experience in both areas are highly encouraged to apply.
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and scientific writing skills.
  • Ability to work independently and mentor junior researchers.

What We Offer

  • A stimulating, collaborative, and well-equipped research environment.
  • Opportunities to work on high-impact, cutting-edge projects.
  • Access to state-of-the-art facilities and computational resources.
  • Strong mentorship and career development support.
  • Opportunities for international collaborations and conference presentations.
  • A competitive salary and benefits package.

How to Apply

Interested candidates are encouraged to send the following documents to [Professor's Email Address]:

  1. A Cover Letter detailing your research interests, relevant experience, and career goals, clearly indicating whether you are applying for a PhD or Postdoctoral position. Please also mention how your interests align with our group's research areas.
  2. A detailed Curriculum Vitae (CV), including a list of publications (if applicable).
  3. Academic Transcripts.
  4. Contact information for 2-3 References.

We are committed to diversity and inclusion and encourage applications from all qualified individuals. Applications will be reviewed on an ongoing basis until the positions are filled.

We look forward to hearing from you and potentially welcoming you to our team!
